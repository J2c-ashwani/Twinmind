## Example Conversation: Same Situation, Different Modes

**User**: "I keep procrastinating on my goals and I don't know why."

### Normal Twin Response:
> "I hear you. Procrastination can be frustrating, especially when you know what you want to achieve. Let's think about this together. What do you think might be holding you back? Is there a particular goal that feels most overwhelming right now?"

### Future Twin Response:
> "I see this pattern, and I want you to imagine something with me. It's one year from now. There are two versions of you: one who broke through this procrastination, and one who didn't. ## Switching Modes

Users can switch modes based on their needs:

**Morning**: Future Twin for goal-setting
**Midday**: Normal Twin for check-ins
**Evening**: Therapist Twin for processing
**When stuck**: Dark Twin for accountability

## Implementation

**File**: `backend/src/services/emotionalStyleAdapter.js`

**Functions**:
- `generateStyleDirectives(styleMode)` - Creates mode-specific AI instructions
- `adjustModifiersForStyle(baseModifiers, styleMode)` - Applies style to modifiers
- `getUserStyleMode(userId)` - Fetches user's preferred mode

**Integration**: Applied in chat routes before generating AI response

## Best Practices

### For Normal Twin
- Be consistently supportive
- Balance honesty with kindness
- Use moderate memory references
- Ask gentle questions

### For Future Twin
- Always connect to long-term vision
- Reference past growth patterns
- Paint vivid future scenarios
- Inspire action through vision

### For Dark Twin
- Be direct, never apologize for honesty
- Call out patterns immediately
- No reassurance unless earned
- Challenge every excuse

### For Therapist Twin
- Create absolute safety
- Use reflective listening
- Explore root causes
- Validate while gently challenging
